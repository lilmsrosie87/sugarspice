Baking with Rosie

I can still smell the sweet potatoe pies down in Texas. The smell awakened my senses... and my stomach!

Baking did not initially come to me naturally. It took a few summers of me spending time in my grandmother's country house cooking for weekly Sunday dinner. My grandmother and aunts made sure to make everything from scratch so you knew you were getting a good meal. One of my favorites is my Auntie Faye's German Chocolate Cake.
